# Final Business Rules

## Launch model
- Service area: Madhupur pilot zones only
- Categories: Bike and E-rickshaw
- Dispatch: nearest approved online driver of the requested category
- Revenue: 15 percent platform commission per completed ride
- Settlement cycle: weekly driver settlement

## Customer policies
- Customer can cancel without fee before driver assignment
- Customer cancellation after arrival may incur a fee
- Cash and UPI are the only launch payment methods
- Complaints must stay linked to a ride

## Driver policies
- Driver must be approved before going live
- Driver must be online and category-matched to receive dispatch
- Repeated cancellation or no-show behavior is grounds for ops review

## Waiting and fare rules
- Bike free waiting: 3 minutes
- E-rickshaw free waiting: 5 minutes
- Waiting charges apply after the free window
- Minimum fare is enforced per category

## Support SLA
- First response target: within 4 hours
- Resolution target: within 24 hours for standard issues
