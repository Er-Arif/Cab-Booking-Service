# Design System

## Product tone
- Friendly, local, and operationally clear
- Optimized for low-friction use in a small city
- Minimal visual noise with strong emphasis on action states

## Color tokens
- Canvas: `#F5F0E5`
- Surface: `#FFFAF0`
- Ink: `#1C140D`
- Muted: `#7A6551`
- Primary action: `#0E7A62`
- Secondary highlight: `#D96C06`
- Danger: `#B23A2F`

## Typography
- Display and body family: Georgia
- Heavy emphasis for action labels, KPI values, and primary navigation
- Keep long explanatory text muted and secondary

## UX rules
- Customer flow should prioritize landmark selection and fast ride choice
- Driver flow should favor large, low-ambiguity actions for accept, arrive, start, and complete
- Admin flow should surface state, alerts, and controls without burying operations in nested screens

## Component guidance
- Cards use rounded corners and warm surfaces
- Chips and tags indicate route category, ride state, or pilot status
- Buttons should be large and high-contrast, especially in driver surfaces
