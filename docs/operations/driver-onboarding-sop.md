# Driver Onboarding SOP

Last updated: April 16, 2026

## Goal
Bring drivers into the Version 1 pilot in a controlled and auditable way.

## Intake checklist
- confirm phone number
- confirm full name
- confirm vehicle category: bike or e-rickshaw
- confirm local compliance documents are available for internal review
- confirm the driver understands Version 1 pilot boundaries and payout process

## Approval flow
1. Open the admin Drivers page.
2. Review the driver's profile and vehicle category.
3. Confirm required compliance information is present in the internal onboarding record.
4. If the driver is approved, update status to approved.
5. If information is missing, keep the driver pending and record the follow-up needed.
6. If the driver fails compliance review, mark the driver rejected and record the reason internally.

## Pilot rules
- do not approve a driver with incomplete identity or vehicle details
- do not allow category mismatch between vehicle type and approved service
- do not promise real-time document-storage workflows until Version 2

## Daily review
- confirm newly approved drivers are visible in the admin list
- confirm rejected or pending drivers have clear internal notes
- confirm active driver count matches pilot expectations
