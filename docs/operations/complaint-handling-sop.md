# Complaint Handling SOP

Last updated: April 16, 2026

## Service targets
- first response target: 4 hours
- resolution target: 24 hours

## Intake sources
- customer complaint submission
- driver issue submission
- operator-observed incident

## Resolution flow
1. Open the Complaints page in the admin panel.
2. Review complaint type, ride reference, timestamp, and description.
3. Cross-check the ride record, payment status, and involved customer and driver.
4. Determine whether the issue is:
   - informational clarification
   - fare dispute
   - behavior or safety issue
   - technical issue
5. Record the resolution note in clear operational language.
6. Mark the complaint resolved only after the internal review is complete.

## Escalate immediately when
- there is a safety threat
- harassment or abuse is alleged
- there is evidence of fraud
- ride records conflict in a way the admin panel cannot explain

## Operator note standard
Each complaint note should capture:
- what happened
- what was verified
- what action was taken
- whether follow-up is still required
