# Version 1 Known Limitations

Last updated: April 16, 2026

## Provider limitations
- OTP remains mock or local rather than live SMS delivery
- maps and routing remain landmark-first with local logic
- push notifications are not yet backed by final production delivery services
- document and file storage are not yet on final production providers
- payments remain cash and UPI record-tracking rather than gateway collection

## Product limitations
- mobile live sync is still simpler than a full production realtime stack
- pilot geography is intentionally limited
- driver onboarding workflows are operationally controlled rather than fully automated
- admin permissions are not yet fine-grained for larger teams

## Operational limitations
- Version 1 assumes a small pilot team and controlled operator oversight
- some procedures rely on manual review and disciplined logging
- support, payout review, and incident handling depend on the handbook and SOP compliance

## Legal limitations
- legal texts are Version 1 pilot templates
- legal review by qualified counsel is still required before a broader commercial launch

## Release limitation
- Version 1 should not be presented as the final production release architecture
