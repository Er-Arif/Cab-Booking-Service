# Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 16, 2026

## 1. Scope
This Privacy Policy explains how the Madhupur Ride Booking Platform collects, uses, stores, and operationally shares data for the customer app, driver app, admin panel, and backend systems.

## 2. Data we collect
We may collect:
- name and phone number
- role and account status
- ride requests, ride history, and complaint records
- pickup and drop landmarks, addresses, notes, and coordinates
- driver availability, trip progress, and earnings records
- authentication and session metadata
- admin actions, reports, and support notes

## 3. Version 1 provider mode
Version 1 may use local, mock, or development adapters for OTP, maps, notifications, payment tracking, and file storage. Data created during those flows may still be stored in platform systems for operational testing, audit, and pilot validation.

## 4. Why we process data
We process data to:
- authenticate users
- estimate fares and create rides
- assign rides and manage trip lifecycle
- handle complaints and support
- generate reports and operational insights
- maintain service security and auditability

## 5. Operational sharing
Data may be visible to:
- the customer using the ride service
- the assigned driver for a ride
- authorized admins and support operators
- approved infrastructure providers used to host the application

## 6. Data retention
Pilot data should be retained only as long as reasonably needed for operations, audits, support, legal review, tax or accounting support, and future Version 2 planning.

## 7. Security measures
The platform uses role-based access, validation, session controls, and backend security middleware. Even so, no system can guarantee perfect security, especially during a pilot.

## 8. User rights and corrections
Before broader public launch, the team should define the final operational process for access, correction, deletion, and retention requests after legal review.

## 9. Children's use
The platform is not intended for unsupervised use by minors where local law or service policy would restrict such use.

## 10. Policy status
This privacy policy is a Version 1 internal template and should be reviewed by qualified legal counsel before public commercial rollout.
