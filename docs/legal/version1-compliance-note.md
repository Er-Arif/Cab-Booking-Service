# Version 1 Compliance Note

Last updated: April 16, 2026

## Purpose
This note records the legal and compliance operating posture for Version 1 of the Madhupur Ride Booking Platform.

## Version 1 assumptions
- Version 1 is a controlled pilot, not a broad public commercial rollout.
- External service dependencies may run through mock, local, or development adapters.
- Legal templates are included so product surfaces can present agreements and privacy disclosures consistently.

## What Version 1 covers
- internal legal templates for terms, privacy, customer, and driver use
- app-level acknowledgement text on sign-in flows
- admin visibility of the legal document set
- explicit documentation of pilot boundaries and mock-provider usage

## What Version 1 does not claim
- final legal sufficiency across all jurisdictions
- transport license compliance outside the defined pilot assumptions
- tax, labor, insurance, or commercial platform compliance review by counsel
- production-grade third-party provider contracts for SMS, maps, payments, or storage

## Required before wider launch
- lawyer review of all user-facing legal text
- review of applicable motor transport, aggregator, labor, privacy, and tax obligations
- final complaint, refund, payout, and data-retention policy approval
- final provider terms review for SMS, maps, payment, notifications, and storage
