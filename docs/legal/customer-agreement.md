# Customer Agreement

Last updated: April 16, 2026

## 1. Customer account responsibilities
Customers agree to:
- provide accurate booking details
- use reachable phone numbers
- be present at pickup points within a reasonable time
- treat drivers respectfully
- avoid misuse, false bookings, or payment disputes made in bad faith

## 2. Booking and ride expectations
- Fare estimates are indicative and may change if trip conditions differ.
- Driver assignment depends on availability, location, service category, and pilot operations.
- Customers should verify pickup and drop details before confirming a ride.

## 3. Payment acknowledgment
- Version 1 supports cash and UPI record-tracking.
- Customers remain responsible for paying the final ride amount accepted through platform operations.

## 4. Cancellation and no-show behavior
- Repeated no-shows, false bookings, abusive behavior, or misuse may lead to account restriction.
- Cancellation policy refinements may be introduced in later versions after pilot review.

## 5. Complaint process
- Customers may submit complaints through the app or support workflow.
- Complaint resolution may require ride details, payment notes, or operator review.

## 6. Consent
By signing in and using the platform, customers acknowledge the Terms and Conditions and Privacy Policy.

## 7. Template status
This agreement is a Version 1 launch template and should be reviewed by qualified legal counsel before broader deployment.
