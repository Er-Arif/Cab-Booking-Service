# Driver Agreement

Last updated: April 16, 2026

## 1. Driver onboarding responsibilities
Drivers agree to:
- provide accurate identity and vehicle information
- maintain required licenses, permits, and local compliance documents
- keep contact details current
- operate only within approved service policies and zones

## 2. Conduct and safety
Drivers must:
- drive lawfully and safely
- treat customers respectfully
- avoid harassment, discrimination, or intimidation
- avoid accepting offline side-deals that bypass pilot policies where prohibited by operations

## 3. Availability and trip operations
- Drivers may set online or offline availability through the platform.
- Once a ride is accepted, drivers should follow the expected trip lifecycle honestly and promptly.
- False completion, false arrival, fare manipulation, or trip misuse may result in suspension.

## 4. Earnings and payments
- Version 1 uses commission-based operational accounting.
- Earnings records, platform commission, and settlement notes may be tracked by the backend and admin panel.
- Final payout procedure must follow the operator handbook and accounting workflow.

## 5. Documents and compliance
- Driver approval may depend on internal review of submitted or recorded compliance information.
- Version 1 may use local or simplified storage and verification flows before Version 2 provider upgrades.

## 6. Complaint and investigation support
Drivers agree to cooperate with reasonable complaint investigation, ride review, and operational audit requests.

## 7. Consent
By signing in and operating on the platform, drivers acknowledge the Terms and Conditions and Privacy Policy and agree to this Driver Agreement.

## 8. Template status
This agreement is a Version 1 launch template and should be reviewed by qualified legal counsel before broader deployment.
