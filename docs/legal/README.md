# Legal Documentation

This folder contains the Version 1 legal and compliance templates for the Madhupur Ride Booking Platform.

## Important notice
- These documents are product-ready internal templates for Version 1.
- They help the team implement consent flows, operator processes, and launch boundaries.
- They are not a substitute for review by a qualified lawyer licensed for the launch jurisdiction.
- Before any public commercial rollout, the team should obtain legal review and replace or revise any clauses that do not fit local law, tax rules, labor rules, transport regulation, or consumer protection requirements.

## Version 1 boundary
- Version 1 uses mock, dev, or local adapters for OTP, maps, notifications, file storage, and payment collection flows where applicable.
- Legal copy must disclose those boundaries internally during pilot planning and externally where public user expectations would otherwise be misleading.
- Version 2 is the planned milestone for real provider integration and final legal review before broader launch.
